Engaging Defenders

How do you take on a defender?

1v1 situations are unavoidable in soccer. Here are some tips to make you an effective dribbler:

  1. Approach your opponent quickly, taking small touches to keep the ball close to you.
  2. Slow down when you get close (about 3-5 feet) to the defender.
  3. Perform the skill move. Make it as convincing as possible.
  4. Suddenly turn the other direction, accelerating past the defender.
  5. Dribble with speed away from the defender.

Key Concepts

Here are some things to keep in mind about dribbling:

  • Change of direction
  • Change of speed
  • Lower your centre of gravity
  • Turn your head and body as part of the fake

Great dribblers have mastery over skill moves and fakes, to the point where they come naturally. These players move the ball extremely efficiently.
